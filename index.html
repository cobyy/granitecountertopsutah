What We Do

Custom Stone Countertops for Every Space

We handle everything from templating to final installation, so you get a perfect fit every time.

Natural Stone

Howard works with the full spectrum of natural stone: granite, quartzite, marble, soapstone, limestone, and onyx. Each material is sourced and selected in person at the slab yard — with you, if you'd like.

Quartz & Porcelain

Engineered surfaces offer consistent color, non-porous durability, and zero maintenance. Howard installs quartz and large-format porcelain slabs for kitchens, bathrooms, and outdoor spaces.
