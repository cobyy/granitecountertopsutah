Stone Materials

The Stones We Work With

Every material has its own personality. Howard will help you find the right match for your space, lifestyle, and budget — and he'll walk you through slab selection in person at the yard.

Granite

The classic choice for a reason. Granite is one of the hardest natural stones on earth — virtually scratch-proof, heat-resistant, and available in an enormous range of colors and patterns. Every slab is genuinely one-of-a-kind, formed over millions of years deep in the earth's crust.

Best for: Kitchen countertops, high-traffic surfaces

Learn More about Granite →

Quartzite

Often mistaken for marble, quartzite gives you that soft, luminous beauty with dramatically more durability. It's a natural metamorphic stone — formed under intense heat and pressure — and it holds up like it. Sophisticated and tough at the same time.

Best for: Kitchens where you want marble aesthetics without the sensitivity

Learn More about Quartzite →

Marble

There's nothing that looks like marble. Its distinctive veining, cool surface, and timeless elegance have made it the stone of palaces and luxury homes for centuries. Our team has placed marble in everything from master bath vanities to the lobby of the Huntsman Cancer Center.

Best for: Bathrooms, fireplace surrounds, statement kitchens

Soapstone

Dark, dense, and remarkably non-porous, soapstone is one of the few natural stones that never needs sealing. It develops a beautiful natural patina over time and is naturally resistant to bacteria, acids, and heat. A favorite for farmhouse and craftsman-style kitchens — and a material Howard has even sourced from Colorado when local yards didn't carry it.

Best for: Kitchens, character-rich interiors

Quartz

Engineered from 90–95% ground natural quartz and polymer resins, quartz surfaces are non-porous, never need sealing, and come in a remarkable range of consistent colors and patterns. If low-maintenance is a priority, quartz belongs near the top of your list.

Best for: Busy kitchens, families with kids, modern aesthetics

Learn More about Quartz →

Onyx

Onyx is nature showing off. Its translucent layers and dramatic color banding — golds, greens, creams, and rust — create surfaces unlike anything else. It's a specialty material best reserved for places where it can truly steal the show. When our team installs onyx, people stop and stare.

Best for: Bar tops, fireplace surrounds, statement accent pieces

Limestone

Warm, matte, and earthy — limestone brings a softness that few stones can match. Our team has installed limestone vanities and shower surrounds that look like they've been part of the home forever. A quieter, more understated elegance that pairs beautifully with natural wood and wrought iron.

Best for: Bathrooms, spa-style showers, Old World and rustic interiors

Porcelain

Modern large-format porcelain slabs offer extraordinary versatility — ultra-thin, incredibly durable, resistant to UV, staining, and heat. They can convincingly replicate marble, concrete, or wood with virtually zero maintenance. A great option for outdoor kitchens and contemporary designs.

Best for: Outdoor kitchens, modern interiors, low-maintenance lifestyles

Where We Install

Every Application, Done Right

Stone isn't just for kitchens. Howard and his team install custom stonework throughout the home — anywhere you want something beautiful and built to outlast everything around it.

Kitchen Countertops

The heart of the home deserves the best material in the house. Our team measures, fabricates, and installs to a perfect fit — sink cutouts, cooktop openings, and custom edge profiles all included.

Bathroom Vanities

Single and double sink tops, custom overhangs, precision undermount cutouts. Stone vanities are one of the fastest ways to transform a bathroom — and they last forever.

Fireplace Surrounds & Hearths

A stone fireplace surround becomes the focal point of every room. Our team has installed everything from simple hearth slabs to full elaborate surrounds in homes across the Wasatch Front.

Mantles

A custom stone mantle is a statement piece that never goes out of style. Howard works with you on dimensions and material to create something that feels architecturally intentional — not tacked on.

Showers & Shower Surrounds

Natural stone showers are more luxurious and easier to maintain than tile grout over time. Our team handles full shower wall installations with precision-cut panels and watertight seams.

Bathtub Surrounds & Tops

Stone bathtub decks and surrounds bring spa-level luxury to almost any tub. Limestone and marble are particularly popular here for their warmth, texture, and visual softness.

Tables & Islands

Kitchen islands, dining table slabs, outdoor tables — our team fabricates custom stone tops for any base. Granite and quartzite are especially popular for outdoor use given their weather resistance.

Custom & Miscellaneous Applications

Bar tops, window sills, laundry room counters, outdoor kitchens, desk surfaces, commercial installations — if it can be made from stone, our team has probably built it. Just call and ask.
