Who We Are

Meet Howard Gerstner

Howard Gerstner

Owner & Master Fabricator

Granite Countertops of Utah LLC is built around one person: Howard Gerstner. With over 25 years of hands-on experience in construction and stone fabrication, Howard has worked on everything from private homes to landmark commercial projects — including the St. Regis Hotel in Deer Valley and the Huntsman Cancer Center.

When you hire us, you get Howard and his dedicated team — from the first phone call through final installation. He'll guide you through slab selection at the yard, consult on all fabrication and installation, and sometimes even help out a little to ensure everything is perfect. Full accountability, no surprises.

Howard works with granite, marble, soapstone, quartz, solid surface, and laminate — whatever your project calls for. His reputation across Salt Lake and Utah County was built almost entirely by word of mouth, and he intends to keep it that way.

If you want to talk countertops, call Howard directly. He picks up.
